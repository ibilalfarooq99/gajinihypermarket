About Us

Welcome to Gajini Hypermarket, your one-stop destination for all your grocery needs. We are dedicated to providing high-quality products and a seamless shopping experience for our customers. At Gajini Hypermarket, we source the freshest and finest groceries to ensure that our customers receive the best products every time. Our wide range of products includes fresh fruits, vegetables, dairy products, meats, and much more. With our commitment to quality, affordability, and convenience, we aim to exceed customer expectations and make their shopping experience enjoyable. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff is always ready to assist you with any queries and help you find the products you need. Shop with us and experience the difference of Gajini Hypermarket, where customer satisfaction is our top priority.

Founded in 2023 by Bilal Farooq, a 21-year-old businessman from Pakistan living in the UAE, Gajini Hypermarket aims to revolutionize the grocery shopping experience. With his passion for entrepreneurship and dedication to customer satisfaction, Bilal established the business to cater to the diverse needs of the local community. As a young and dynamic entrepreneur, Bilal Farooq has been actively involved in the operations and growth of Gajini Hypermarket.
